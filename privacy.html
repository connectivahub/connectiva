Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

1. Introduction

ConnectivaHub ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you from various sources:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, service address
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, clicks, navigation patterns
  • Service Information: Issue details, equipment types, troubleshooting history
  • Communication Data: Call recordings (with consent), emails, support tickets

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing technical support and resolving connectivity issues
  • Improving our services and website functionality
  • Communicating with you about your account and services
  • Sending promotional communications (with your consent)
  • Complying with legal obligations and fraud prevention
  • Analyzing usage patterns to enhance user experience

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may disclose information only in these circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist us in operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • When legally required by law enforcement or court orders
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • With your explicit consent

5. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information including encryption, secure socket layer (SSL) technology, and limited access controls. However, no security system is impenetrable. We recommend strong passwords and secure communication practices.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve user experience. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. We do not use tracking technologies for profiling without consent.

7. Retention of Information

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion of your data at any time, subject to legal retention requirements.

8. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request removal of your data
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Data Portability: Receive your information in portable format

9. Children's Privacy

ConnectivaHub does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover we've collected information from a child, we will promptly delete it.

10. CCPA and State Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act. Contact us for details on exercising your rights.

11. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union, we comply with General Data Protection Regulation requirements. We process data based on your consent, contract necessity, or legal obligations.
