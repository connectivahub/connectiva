## Image Optimization

All images are loaded with:
- **Format**: WebP via Unsplash CDN
- **Width**: 1600px for full sections, 800px for cards
- **Quality**: 80 (q=80 parameter)
- **Library**: ixlib=rb-4.0.3

## Future Image Updates
