## 🎨 Color Scheme

### Background
- **Base Image**: Fiber optic network (blue tones)
- **Overlay Gradient**: Deep blue → Dark blue → Black
- **Opacity**: 95% → 85% → 70%

### Text Colors
- **Title "Connected"**: Blue-to-gold gradient
- **Badge**: Semi-transparent blue with cyan border
- **Body Text**: White with 80% opacity
- **Stats Numbers**: Blue-to-gold gradient
- **Stats Labels**: White with 60% opacity

### Buttons
- **Primary**: Blue gradient + glow shadow
- **Secondary**: Transparent with white border + glassmorphism

### Cards (Desktop only)
- **Background**: White 5% + glass blur
- **Border**: White 10%
- **Hover Border**: Cyan 30%
- **Icons**: Emoji (📶 ⚡ ✓)
- **Title**: White
- **Status**: Cyan blue

## ✨ Animations & Effects

### On Page Load
1. **Left content**: Slides in from left (1s)
2. **Right cards**: Slides in from right (1s)
3. **Fade in**: Everything fades up

### Continuous Animations
1. **Cards**: Float up and down (3s infinite)
   - Card 1: No delay
   - Card 2: 0.5s delay
   - Card 3: 1s delay
2. **Scroll indicator**: Bounce up and down (2s infinite)

### On Hover
1. **Cards**: Lift 5px + glow shadow
2. **Primary button**: Lift 2px + enhanced shadow
3. **Secondary button**: Background fade in

## 📱 Responsive Breakpoints

### Large Desktop (>1440px)
- Maximum width: 1400px
- Full spacing and sizing

### Desktop (1024px - 1440px)
- Standard split layout
- Normal spacing

### Tablet (768px - 1024px)
- Reduced spacing
- Smaller title (4rem)
- Scaled down cards

### Mobile (480px - 768px)
- **Single column**
- Right cards hidden
- Vertical CTAs
- Title: 3rem

### Small Mobile (<480px)
- **Compact layout**
- Vertical stats
- Title: 2.2rem
- Full-width buttons

## 💡 Design Tips

### What Makes This Hero Effective:

1. **Immediate Value**: Badge + headline communicate service instantly
2. **Social Proof**: Stats build trust immediately
3. **Clear CTAs**: Two options for different user intentions
4. **Visual Interest**: Animated cards keep attention
5. **Modern Aesthetic**: Gradients, glass effects, smooth animations
6. **Tech-Focused**: Image and colors match connectivity theme
7. **Mobile-First**: Cleanly adapts to all screen sizes

### Conversion Optimizations:
- ✅ Primary CTA stands out (blue glow)
- ✅ Secondary CTA for research-phase users
- ✅ Stats reduce friction (trust signals)
- ✅ Clear value proposition in headline
- ✅ Service examples visible (cards)
