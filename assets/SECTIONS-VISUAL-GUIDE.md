## 🎨 Color & Style Guide

### Testimonials Visual Style

**Cards**
- Background: Frosted glass effect (white 3% + blur)
- Border: Soft white glow (10%)
- Hover: Cyan glow appears
- Featured: Gradient border (blue → gold)

**Text**
- Stars: Gold color (#d4af37)
- Quote: White 85%, italic
- Name: White 100%, bold
- Role: White 60%, small

**Avatar**
- Circle with gradient background
- White initials centered
- 50px diameter

### FAQ Visual Style

**Accordion Cards**
- Background: Subtle cyan tint (3%)
- Border: Cyan outline (15%)
- Hover: Border brightens to 30%
- Active: Expanded with smooth animation

**Text**
- Question: White, bold
- Answer: White 80%
- Icon: Cyan blue (+/×)

**Interactions**
- Click question → expands answer
- Plus (+) rotates to X (×)
- Only one FAQ open at a time
- Smooth height transition

## 💡 Pro Tips

### For Best Visual Impact

1. **Testimonials**
   - Keep quotes authentic and specific
   - Use real initials/names
   - Vary the customer roles
   - Highlight 1-2 as "featured"

2. **FAQ**
   - Put most important questions first
   - Keep answers concise (2-3 lines)
   - Use active voice
   - Include specific numbers/facts

3. **Spacing**
   - Let sections breathe
   - Don't overcrowd on mobile
   - Maintain visual hierarchy
