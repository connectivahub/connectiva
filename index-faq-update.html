Important Questions

Transparency and clarity about our services

Are you an official cable or internet service provider?

+

No. We are an independent third-party assistance platform launched in 2026. We are not an ISP and do not provide internet or cable services.

Do you sell internet, cable, or streaming plans?

+

No. We provide guidance and inform ational assistance only. We do not sell or activate any service plans.

Do you charge for your services?

+

Yes. Our service fees are separate from provider charges. We charge for our independent assistance, guidance, and support services.

Will I still be billed by my service provider?

+

Yes. Provider billing remains unchanged. Our fees are separate. You continue to pay your ISP/cable provider directly for their services.

Do you need my account passwords or sensitive information?

+

No. We never request provider passwords, account login credentials, or other sensitive information. We provide guidance only.

Which providers do you support?

+

We may reference multiple providers for guidance purposes only. We have no affiliation, partnership, or endorsement from any telecom, cable, or internet service provider.

Are your services refundable?

+

Yes. Refund requests can be submitted within 7 to 15 days of service purchase, depending on the type of assistance provided. Eligibility depends on service type and whether assistance has already been delivered. Please review our Refund Policy page for full details.

How quickly can you assist me?

+

Most customers connect with an independent advisor within 5 minutes. Our average response time varies based on call volume and the complexity of your connectivity question.
